Describes subscription billing, where a company automatically charges customers each cycle (monthly, yearly, etc.) for access to a product or service. Notes that 20-40% of churn in recurring businesses comes from involuntary payment failures rather than dissatisfaction, and recommends automated retry mechanisms, flexible pricing, and integrated payment infrastructure to minimize revenue loss.
