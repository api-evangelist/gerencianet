Explains recurring credit, the charging method where the customer is automatically debited each cycle (monthly, quarterly, or annually) without the full service value compromising the card limit at once. Describes how the model turns unpredictable sales into a continuous automated revenue stream, benefiting SaaS, schools, and service providers with predictable cash flow and reduced defaults.
